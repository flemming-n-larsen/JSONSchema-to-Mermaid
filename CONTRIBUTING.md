# Contributing to JSONSchema-to-Mermaid

Thank you for your interest in this project!

At this moment, the project is in early development and we are not yet
accepting external Pull Requests while we finalize the core architecture
and licensing model.

### Future Contributions
We plan to open for contributions soon. Please note that to maintain the
ability to offer commercial licenses (to fund the project's development),
contributors will eventually be asked to sign a Contributor License
Agreement (CLA).

If you have ideas or find bugs, please feel free to open an **Issue** to discuss them!
